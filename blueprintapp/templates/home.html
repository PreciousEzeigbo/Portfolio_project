About FitConnect

FitConnect was born out of my personal fitness struggles. As someone who hasn't been to the gym yet (yes, you read that right!), I was looking for a way to stay active and hold myself accountable, all while connecting with others on a similar journey.

The result? FitConnect, a place where you can track progress, stay motivated, and eventually, flex your social muscles (pun intended). Also, if you've ever found yourself wondering whether walking from the fridge to the couch counts as cardio – you’re in the right place

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, developed with dedication and expertise. Learn more about Holberton School here.
